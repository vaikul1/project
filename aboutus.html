About Us

“ Success First is the most innovative online job portal in India.Success First connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, Success First focuses on the entire career growth of candidates. Shine works closely to bridge the gap between talent & opportunities and offers end-to-end recruitment solutions. Shine Job Fair brings candidates and top employers under one roof. ”



Features for Employers:


Provide space to Employers where they can manage company profile, post their vacant job ads and search the relevant database of jobseekers’ resume. Provide flexibility to edit the posts by companies and offers subscription management. Filter job seekers on the basis of skills, experience, education etc. Acknowledgement mail is sent to job seekers to continue recruitment process.

Features for Job Seekers:


Provide facilities of filling registration forms. Provide space to post resume and offer flexibility for editing. Upload/attach image in the resume. Search entire job listing and apply for desired jobs. Jobs bookmarking and notification/subscription facility. View employers’ statistics and filter them on the basis of reputation, salary range etc.
